About Me

My name is Poom Kritpracha. I am an undergraduate senior studying computational and applied mathematics at Rice University. For my undergraduate project, I am currently working on the implementation of a zero-dimensional solute transport model for the circulatory system under the supervision of Dr. Beatrice Riviere and Dr. Charles Puelz. I have a general interest in dynamical systems, computational physiology, and numerical linear algebra.
